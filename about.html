About InstantQR

InstantQR is a free online QR code generator designed to make creating QR codes fast, simple, and accessible to everyone.

Our tool allows you to generate QR codes for:

  • Website URLs
  • WiFi networks
  • Text messages
  • Business use cases

InstantQR runs entirely in your browser. We do not store or save the QR codes you generate.

Our mission is to provide a clean, secure, and easy-to-use QR code generator for individuals and businesses.

If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to contact us.
