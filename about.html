What is InstantQR.io?

InstantQR.io is a free, lightweight tool hub built to help you get quick results without clutter. We offer a QR code generator (WiFi and URL), plus practical utilities like converters, crypto calculators, and security tools — designed to work smoothly on both mobile and desktop.

What you can do on InstantQR

🔳 QR Code Generators

Create WiFi QR codes for guests and URL QR codes for websites, menus, flyers, and business cards.

📏 Converters & Everyday Tools

Convert common units quickly and use simple utilities that save time — built for speed and clarity.

⛽ Crypto & Web3 Utilities

Estimate gas fees and calculate crypto profit/loss with straightforward tools made for traders and builders.

🔐 Security Utilities

Generate strong passwords and use privacy-minded tools for everyday online safety.

Privacy-first

We aim to keep InstantQR privacy-first. Many tools are designed to run locally in your browser. We don’t require accounts and we don’t intentionally store sensitive inputs such as WiFi details, passwords, or text you enter.

Who InstantQR is for

  • Small businesses creating QR codes for menus, signs, and promotions
  • Events and venues sharing WiFi access instantly
  • Creators and teams needing fast conversions and time tools
  • Crypto users estimating gas and calculating trade outcomes
