What is InstantQR?

InstantQR.io is a lightweight QR code generator for Text/URL and Wi-Fi. It’s designed to be simple, quick, and usable on mobile and desktop.

Privacy-first

QR generation happens locally in your browser. We don’t ask you to create an account and we don’t store the data you type into the generator.

Common uses

  • Share a website or menu link
  • Create Wi-Fi QR codes for guests
  • Add QR codes to flyers, business cards, and events
