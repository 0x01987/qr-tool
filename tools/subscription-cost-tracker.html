Frequently asked questions

What is a subscription cost tracker?

A subscription cost tracker is a tool that helps you list recurring services like Netflix, HBO Max, Spotify, software plans and memberships so you can see your total monthly and yearly cost in one place.

Can I track yearly and monthly subscriptions together?

Yes. This tool supports monthly, yearly, quarterly and weekly billing cycles. It automatically converts them into monthly and yearly equivalents for a cleaner comparison.

Does this subscription tracker store my data online?

No. Your data is stored locally in your browser using local storage. No sign-in is required, and nothing needs to be sent to a server for the tracker to work.

Can I use this for more than streaming subscriptions?

Yes. You can track streaming services, software plans, cloud storage, gym memberships, internet plans, gaming subscriptions and almost any recurring expense.
