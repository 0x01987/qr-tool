About this fee tool

Bitcoin uses sat/vB-style fee rates, Litecoin, Dogecoin, and Dash usually expose per-kilobyte fee estimates, and TRON uses resource pricing based on bandwidth and energy. This tool adapts to each fee model so you can estimate cost more accurately without switching between multiple pages.

Frequently Asked Questions

Which networks does this fee estimator support?

It supports Bitcoin, Litecoin, Dogecoin, Dash, and TRON. The calculator uses the fee model that best fits each supported network.

Why are these networks estimated differently?

Bitcoin-style chains often price transactions by size, while TRON uses resource-based pricing through bandwidth and energy. That is why the inputs and results differ across networks.

Is this the exact final fee?

No. It is an estimate based on live network data and your selected values. Final wallet fees can vary due to wallet behavior, transaction complexity, and network congestion.

Do you store my endpoint URLs or inputs?

No. The tool runs in your browser. Requests go directly from your device to the selected endpoint.
