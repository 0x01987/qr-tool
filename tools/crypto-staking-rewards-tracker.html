What this tool does well

  • Tracks many major staking assets in one place
  • Uses live token prices for quick portfolio valuation
  • Lets you enter your own APR or APY per position
  • Supports compounding assumptions for reward projections

Best use cases

Validator dashboards, exchange staking positions, liquid staking holdings, DeFi staking snapshots, and personal crypto reward planning.

How to use it

  • Add a position and pick an asset preset
  • Enter your staked amount
  • Use APR or APY based on your staking provider
  • Choose compound frequency if you restake rewards
  • Refresh prices to update USD values

Important note

This tool is a portfolio estimator. Exact staking outcomes vary by validator uptime, slashing risk, lock rules, reward schedules, fees, protocol mechanics, and claim or restake timing.

FAQ

Does this staking rewards tracker use live prices?

Yes. It can fetch live prices for supported assets and combine them with your staking inputs.

Does InstantQR store my staking portfolio?

No. Portfolio data stays in your browser and is saved locally on your device unless you export it yourself.

Can I enter my own APR or APY?

Yes. Each row supports editable APR or APY so you can match your provider’s current reward rate.

Does this replace chain-specific validator analytics?

No. This is a broad rewards tracker. Some networks have validator-specific APIs and dashboards for exact on-chain reward accounting.
