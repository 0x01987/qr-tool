How conversion works

Fahrenheit and Celsius are different temperature scales, so this conversion uses an offset and a ratio. It is not just a simple multiplier.

Why use this tool

FAQ

How do you convert Fahrenheit to Celsius?

Use the formula °C = (°F − 32) × 5/9.

What is 32°F in Celsius?

32°F equals 0°C.

Can I also convert Celsius to Fahrenheit here?

Yes. This tool supports both directions. You can type into either field and it will convert automatically.

Does this tool store my temperature values?

No. This tool runs locally in your browser and does not store your entries.
