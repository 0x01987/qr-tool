How this calculator works

This crypto profit calculator compares your buy price and sell price, then calculates gross profit or loss, total fees, cost basis, net proceeds, net profit or loss, ROI percentage, break-even sell price, and an optional tax estimate on positive gains.

You can use quantity mode if you know how many coins or tokens you bought, or investment mode if you only know how much money you originally invested.

Why use this crypto ROI calculator?

Manual profit math becomes harder once you include exchange fees, flat costs, and break-even targets. This page makes that much faster and gives you a cleaner view of what your trade actually returned.

  • Estimate net crypto profit after buy and sell fees
  • Compare ROI quickly across different trades
  • See break-even sell price before exiting a position
  • Add optional tax estimate for planning

Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate crypto profit and ROI?

Enter buy price and sell price, then choose quantity mode or investment mode. The calculator shows net profit or loss after optional fees, plus ROI and break-even sell price.

Does this calculator include fees?

Yes. You can add optional buy and sell fees as either a percentage or a flat amount.

Is my data sent to a server?

No. This tool runs locally in your browser and your inputs are not uploaded.
