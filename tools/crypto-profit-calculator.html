FAQ

What does this crypto calculator include?

This calculator includes buy price, sell price, quantity, fees, total cost, total proceeds, net profit or loss, ROI, and break-even price.

Can I include exchange fees?

Yes. You can include buy fees and sell fees to get a more accurate profit or loss result.

Does this tool run locally?

Yes. All calculations run locally in your browser.
