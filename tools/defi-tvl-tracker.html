DeFi TVL Tracker FAQ

What can I use this DeFi TVL tracker for?

Use it to monitor protocol and chain TVL, compare categories, inspect short-term changes, and quickly identify where capital is concentrated in DeFi.

Why include a proxy-ready setup?

A proxy or serverless cache improves reliability, avoids browser-side rate limits, and gives you a stable source even when public endpoints change behavior.

Is this mobile friendly?

Yes. The layout is optimized for small screens, fast taps, readable cards, and smooth table scrolling on phones and tablets.
