FAQ

What is a vCard QR code?

A vCard QR code stores contact information like name, phone, email, company, website, and address so people can scan and save it quickly.

Can I download a VCF file?

Yes. This tool can export your contact data as a .vcf file for sharing and importing into contacts.

Can I share the contact with a link?

Yes. This tool generates a hash-based share link that restores contact fields in the browser.

Does this tool store my contact info?

No. This version builds the contact and QR code in your browser.
