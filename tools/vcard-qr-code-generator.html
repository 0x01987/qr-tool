FAQ

What is a vCard QR code?

A vCard QR code stores contact information such as name, phone number, email, company, website, and address. When scanned, it can be saved directly to a phone's contacts.

Does this vCard QR generator store my contact details?

No. This tool is browser-based and generates the QR code locally in your browser.

Can I download the QR code?

Yes. You can download the generated vCard QR code as a PNG image.

What contact fields can I include?

You can include first name, last name, organization, title, phone, email, website, address, city, state, postal code, country, and notes.
