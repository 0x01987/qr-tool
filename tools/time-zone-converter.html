Frequently asked questions

Is this time zone converter free?
Yes. It is free to use.
Does it support IANA time zones?
Yes. It supports official zones like America/Phoenix, Europe/London, and Asia/Tokyo.
Does it store my inputs?
No. Conversions run locally in your browser.
Can I compare UTC offsets?
Yes. The result area shows the time difference and UTC offsets for the selected time.

Why use this tool?

This converter is useful for international meetings, remote work, travel planning, customer support, and comparing local time across regions. It is designed to be fast, mobile friendly, privacy-first, and easy to use.
