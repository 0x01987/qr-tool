FAQ

Is this time zone converter free to use?

Yes. The InstantQR time zone converter is free to use.

Does this tool support IANA time zones?

Yes. The tool supports official IANA time zones such as America/Phoenix, Europe/London, and Asia/Tokyo.

Does the time zone converter store my data?

No. Conversions run locally in your browser and no time data is intentionally stored or sent anywhere.

Can I compare UTC offsets between time zones?

Yes. The tool shows the offset difference and each selected time zone's UTC offset for the chosen time.
