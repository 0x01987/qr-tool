How this cups to grams converter works

This converter uses the US cup measurement and assumes water as the reference ingredient. Since 1 US cup is approximately 236.588 milliliters and water has a near 1:1 milliliter-to-gram relationship, the conversion is straightforward.

That makes this tool reliable for water and useful as a quick baseline for general kitchen math. For dry ingredients and thicker liquids, actual grams per cup can vary significantly.

Frequently asked questions

How many grams are in 1 US cup?

This converter uses water as the reference, so 1 US cup is approximately 236.588 grams.

Is this cups to grams conversion exact?

It is accurate for water using the US cup volume. For other ingredients such as flour, sugar, butter, and oats, grams per cup depend on density.

Why do ingredient conversions vary?

Different ingredients pack differently and have different densities. A cup of flour does not weigh the same as a cup of sugar or a cup of butter.

Does this tool store my data?

No. The conversion runs locally in your browser.
