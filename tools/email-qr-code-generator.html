What Is an Email QR Code?

An email QR code is a scannable code that opens the email app with a recipient address and, if you choose, a prefilled subject and message. It is a simple way to move someone from a printed sign, card, brochure, poster, package insert, or support sheet into a ready-to-send email draft.

This is especially useful when email is the preferred contact channel and you want to reduce typing friction. It can help visitors, customers, attendees, or prospects contact you faster and with more complete information.

Where email QR codes work well

  • Support and help desk contact points
  • Sales inquiries and lead follow-up
  • Event registration and sponsor contact
  • Printed brochures and product inserts
  • Business cards and office signage
  • Service request and appointment workflows

Best practices

  • Use a valid email address people can trust
  • Write a clear subject line that guides the reply
  • Keep the prefilled message short and useful
  • Test on multiple phones before printing
  • Use a clear call to action like “Email us now”
