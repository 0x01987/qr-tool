Why Use a Coupon QR Code?

  • Promote discounts faster in print and digital campaigns
  • Make offers easy to scan at events, stores, and restaurants

Best Practices

  • Always include an expiration date when relevant.

FAQ

What is a coupon QR code?

A coupon QR code is a scannable QR code that contains a promo URL or coupon details like offer name, code, expiration date, and terms.

Should I use text mode or URL mode?

Use URL mode if you want people to open a landing page or checkout page. Use text mode if you want the coupon details stored directly in the QR code.

Can I create a coupon QR code without a website?

Yes. Text mode lets you encode the offer, promo code, expiration date, and terms without needing a website.

Does InstantQR store my coupon details?

No. This tool is privacy-first and runs in your browser. It does not intentionally store your input.
