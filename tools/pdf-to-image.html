FAQ

Can this tool convert PDF pages into images?

Yes. You can upload a PDF and export one page or multiple pages as PNG or JPG images.

Can this tool turn images into a PDF?

Yes. You can upload one or more JPG, PNG, or WebP images and combine them into a single PDF file.

Does this tool upload my files to a server?

This tool is designed for browser-side processing. Your files are handled locally in your browser during conversion.

What output formats are supported?

For PDF to image, you can export PNG or JPG. For image to PDF, you can generate a PDF document from one or more images.
