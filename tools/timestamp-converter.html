Frequently asked questions

Can this tool detect seconds and milliseconds automatically?
Yes. It auto-detects whether the timestamp is in seconds or milliseconds.
Can I convert a date back to Unix time?
Yes. Enter a date and time to get Unix timestamps in seconds and milliseconds.
What output formats are shown?

Does this timestamp converter run locally?
Yes. Conversions happen in your browser for privacy and speed.

Why use this tool?

This timestamp converter is useful for API debugging, database work, logs, JWT claims, analytics events, development, and backend systems that use Unix or epoch time. It makes it easy to go from timestamps to readable dates and back again without leaving your browser.
