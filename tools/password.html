FAQ

Do generated passwords leave my device?

No. Password generation happens locally in your browser on your device.

What password length should I use?

Use at least 16 characters for most accounts. For sensitive accounts, use 20 to 32 characters when supported.

Should I use symbols in my password?

Yes, if the website allows them. If not, disable symbols and increase password length instead.

What does avoid ambiguous characters mean?

It removes look-alike characters like O and 0, l and 1, or I and l to reduce mistakes when typing or sharing a password.
