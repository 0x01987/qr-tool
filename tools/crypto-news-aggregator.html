Frequently asked questions

How does this crypto news aggregator work?

It fetches headlines from multiple crypto RSS feeds, normalizes the data, removes duplicates, and displays everything in a filterable dashboard.

Can I search for specific crypto topics?

Yes. Use the search box for keywords like Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, SEC, ETF, DeFi, staking, exchange, wallet, exploit, or any token or market topic.

Are saved stories stored in an account?

No. Saved stories are stored only in your browser using localStorage in this version.

Why might a source fail temporarily?

Some publishers or feed proxies may rate-limit requests, update their feed structure, or return errors. The page includes fallbacks and partial results will still load when possible.
