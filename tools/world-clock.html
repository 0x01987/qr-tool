FAQ

Does this world clock run locally?

Yes. The world clock runs in your browser using built-in time zone support. No login is required.

Can I add or remove cities?

Yes. Add cities or time zones from the search box and remove any clock. Your selected clocks are saved locally in your browser.

Can I change the color theme?

Yes. You can switch between blue, green, red, and white themes.

Does the map show live time directly?

Yes. Each selected city can display its current local time directly in the map label, and clicking the marker opens more detail.
