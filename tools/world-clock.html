Frequently asked questions

Can I change the color theme?
Yes. You can switch between blue, green, red, and white themes.
Can I maximize one clock?
Yes. Each clock card has a maximize button to expand it.
Can I maximize all clocks?
Yes. Use the Maximize All button to expand the whole grid area.

Yes. Selected clocks, time format, and theme are stored locally in your browser.
