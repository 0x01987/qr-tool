FAQ

Can I change the QR code color?

Yes. You can customize both the QR foreground color and the background color.

Can I add a logo in the center?

Yes. You can upload a logo image and place it in the center of the QR code preview and PNG export.

Can I create multiple QR codes at once?

Yes. Batch mode creates one QR code for each non-empty line in your input.

Can I export SVG?

Yes. Single mode supports SVG export, which is useful for designers and print workflows.
