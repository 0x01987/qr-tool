How it’s calculated

Gross P/L = (Sell Price − Buy Price) × Quantity
Fees can be flat or percent, applied to buy and sell totals.
Net P/L = Net Proceeds − Cost Basis
ROI = Net P/L ÷ Cost Basis × 100%

Break-even price estimates the sell price needed so net P/L ≈ 0 (including fees).

FAQ
