What is a QR Code?

A QR code (Quick Response code) is a scannable barcode that stores information like website URLs, Wi-Fi credentials, or text. Most modern smartphone cameras can scan QR codes instantly.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select the QR type (Text/URL or Wi-Fi).
  2. Enter your information.
  3. Click Generate.
  4. Click Download PNG.
