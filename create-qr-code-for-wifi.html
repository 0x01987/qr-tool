Step-by-step: Create a WiFi QR Code

  1. Enter your Wi-Fi name (SSID).
  2. Select your security type (usually WPA).
  3. Enter your Wi-Fi password (unless you choose “No password”).
  4. Click Generate and download the PNG.

Where to use it

  • Home guest access (print and place near the router)
  • Airbnb / rentals (welcome card)
  • Cafes and restaurants (counter sign)
  • Offices (reception desk)
  • Events (check-in table)

Tips for best results

  • Use at least 320px for phone scanning.
  • Choose error correction M or H if printing.
  • Always test-scan before printing many copies.

Privacy note: InstantQR generates QR codes locally in your browser — we do not store your Wi-Fi details.
