InstantQR.io is a privacy-first online tool platform providing QR code generators, converters, and utility tools. We are committed to transparency regarding how information is handled.

Data entered into our QR code generators (such as text, URLs, or Wi-Fi credentials) is processed locally in your browser where possible. We do not intentionally store, transmit, or retain the content you input into our tools.

This information is aggregated and used solely to improve performance and user experience.

5. Cookies

Cookies may be used for:

You can control cookies through your browser settings.

7. Data Security

We aim to follow industry best practices to maintain website security. However, no online platform can guarantee absolute security.
