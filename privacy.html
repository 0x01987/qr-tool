Summary

InstantQR.io generates QR codes directly in your browser. We do not store the text/URL/Wi-Fi details you enter into the QR generator.

Information you provide

The data you type (e.g., URL, text, Wi-Fi SSID/password) stays in your browser. It is used only to generate a QR code on your device.

Cookies

Cookies may be used for analytics and advertising. Where required (EEA/UK/Switzerland), a consent message may be shown before ads load.

If we link to other websites, their privacy practices apply. Please review their policies separately.
