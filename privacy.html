1. Overview

InstantQR.io is a privacy-first online tool platform that provides QR code generators, SEO tools, DNS and network tools, converters, security tools, crypto and Web3 utilities, developer helpers, guides, and other web-based resources. This policy explains what information may be processed when you use the site and how we approach privacy, analytics, advertising, and security.

Our goal is to keep tools simple, useful, and accessible without unnecessary friction. In many cases, that means designing tools to work directly in the browser where possible instead of requiring account creation or persistent storage of user input.

2. Tool Input and User Content

Many InstantQR tools accept user-provided input such as text, URLs, Wi-Fi credentials, contact details, domain names, IP addresses, JSON, timestamps, or other tool-specific values. Where technically possible, that input is processed locally in your browser.

We do not intentionally collect, store, or retain the content you enter into tools simply for using them. However, some tools may rely on third-party services, network lookups, or browser requests in order to function. When that happens, the relevant input may be transmitted as part of the request required to return a result.

Examples of local processing

QR generation, text formatting, conversions, and some browser-based utilities can often run directly on your device without needing permanent storage.

Examples of network-based processing

DNS lookups, IP lookups, external fee lookups, some scam-checking workflows, and similar network tools may need to contact outside services or endpoints to return a result.

3. Analytics

We may use analytics services such as Google Analytics to understand how the site is used, which pages are helpful, which tools are popular, and where usability improvements are needed. Analytics information may include:

  • Pages visited and navigation behavior
  • Device type, browser, and operating environment
  • General geographic region or country-level location signals
  • Referral source or traffic channel
  • Approximate performance data such as page timing

This information is generally used in aggregated form to improve performance, reliability, layout, search visibility, and user experience. It is not intended to identify you personally.

4. Advertising and Google AdSense

InstantQR.io may display advertising through Google AdSense or related advertising partners. These services may use cookies, browser identifiers, or similar technologies to help serve ads, measure ad performance, prevent fraud, and determine whether ads should be personalized or contextual.

Depending on your region, you may be shown a consent notice or ad preference flow before certain advertising technologies are used. Users in places such as the EEA, UK, and Switzerland may receive additional consent choices as required by applicable regulations.

Advertisers and third-party ad providers may apply their own privacy practices. For more information, users should also review Google’s advertising and privacy resources.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

Cookies and related technologies may be used on InstantQR.io for a few common purposes:

  • Analytics and performance measurement
  • Advertising delivery and ad performance measurement
  • Security, abuse prevention, and fraud detection
  • Basic site functionality or user experience improvements

You can usually manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Disabling some cookies may affect how parts of the website or advertising functions behave.

6. Third-Party Services and External Links

Some pages may link to third-party websites, documentation, services, APIs, or external resources. In addition, some tools may depend on external services in order to retrieve results. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party websites or providers.

If you leave InstantQR.io or interact with an outside service from one of our tools, you should review the privacy policy of that third party separately.

7. Security

We aim to follow reasonable security practices to maintain the integrity and safety of the site. That includes efforts to reduce abuse, keep pages functional, and avoid unnecessary data exposure. However, no website, browser session, or online service can guarantee absolute security.

Users should avoid entering highly sensitive personal or financial information into any online tool unless they fully understand how that tool works and are comfortable with the risk profile of browser-based internet use.

8. Data Retention

Because many tools are designed to work without account creation and often process inputs locally where possible, InstantQR.io does not aim to build long-term user profiles around tool usage. Some limited technical logs, analytics signals, or advertising-related records may still exist through site infrastructure, analytics providers, hosting environments, or ad services for operational, security, or reporting purposes.

9. Children’s Privacy

InstantQR.io is not specifically directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly design the site to collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted personal information through the site inappropriately, please use the Contact page to report the issue.

10. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time as tools change, new services are added, analytics or advertising practices evolve, or legal requirements change. When updates are made, the effective date on this page will be revised to reflect the current version.

11. Contact and Privacy Questions

If you have a privacy-related question, notice wording issue, or concern about how a tool works, please visit the Contact page. When reporting an issue, it helps to include the page URL and a short description of the relevant tool or workflow.
