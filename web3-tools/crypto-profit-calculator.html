How it’s calculated
Gross P/L = (Sell Price − Buy Price) × Quantity
Buy fee and sell fee can each be set as a percent or flat amount.
Cost basis = Buy Total + Buy Fee
Net proceeds = Sell Total − Sell Fee
Net P/L = Net Proceeds − Cost Basis
ROI = Net P/L ÷ Cost Basis × 100%

Break-even sell price estimates the price needed so net profit/loss is approximately zero after fees.

About this crypto calculator

This crypto profit calculator helps traders and investors estimate net profit or loss from a trade using buy price, sell price, quantity or investment amount, optional fees, and optional tax rate.

It is useful for Bitcoin, Ethereum, Solana, and other crypto assets when you want a quick ROI estimate without sending your inputs to a server.

Best practices
  • Always include realistic buy and sell fees for better accuracy.
  • Use quantity mode when you know the exact number of coins or tokens.
  • Use investment mode when you only know how much money was spent.
  • Compare break-even price before exiting a position.
